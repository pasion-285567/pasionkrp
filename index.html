About Me

The Melodic Synthesist

I’m Kurt Raymund Pasion — a developer by discipline, a musician at heart. I live between logic and lyric, shifting from code to chord, from syntax to song. To me, code is more than commands, and music is more than sound — both are ways I make sense of the world around me. I build systems like I build melodies: with structure, with soul, and with something honest underneath. As a melodic synthesist, I find rhythm in reason, and beauty in the balance between machine and meaning.
